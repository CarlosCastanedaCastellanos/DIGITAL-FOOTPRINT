Â¿What is a digital footprint?

The digital footprint is the trace you leave when browsing the Internet. Every time you click or like on social networks, or when you use an application from your cell phone or computer, you leave personal information.

TIPS FOR SAVE YOUR DIGITAL FOOTPRINT

Do not save your passwords on shared computers or systems. Anyone could have access to your accounts. Be careful with the photos or videos you upload to the network. Even if you delete material, everything leaves a mark and can be recovered by digital hackers.

DRAWBACKS

Along with the benefits of digital fingerprinting, we also have the following disadvantages: The use of tracking cookies and trackers to follow users while they browse the internet and the risks this implies for users' digital privacy.
